Privacy Policy for blinq

Last Updated: July 6, 2025

blinq Capital LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates blinq (the "Application"), a Python-based automated trading application for buying and selling stocks and options via the Alpaca Trading API, accessible at https://3.blinq.capital. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Application.

1. No Data Collection

The Application does not collect, store, or process any personal or non-personal data, including API credentials, usage data, or trading activity. All data, such as Alpaca API Key ID and Secret Key, is used solely in memory during a request to achieve the desired trading output and is never stored, logged, or persisted in any form.

2. How We Use Information

We use data only during runtime to:

  • Facilitate connectivity with the Alpaca API for executing trades.
  • No data is retained after the request is completed.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data, as none is collected or stored. Data sent to the Alpaca API is subject to Alpaca’s Privacy Policy (available at https://alpaca.markets/disclosures).

4. Data Storage and Security

  • No Storage: The Application does not store any data, personal or otherwise, on our servers or any other medium.
  • Security: API credentials are handled in memory during runtime and are immediately discarded after use. You are responsible for securely managing your Alpaca API keys.
  • Third-Party Handling: Data transmitted to Alpaca is governed by Alpaca’s security practices. Review their policies at https://alpaca.markets/disclosures.

5. Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Securely managing your Alpaca API Key ID and Secret Key.
  • Reviewing Alpaca’s Privacy Policy to understand how your data is handled by their platform.
  • Ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Application does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Any cookies or tracking by Alpaca are governed by their Privacy Policy.

7. Third-Party Services

The Application relies on the Alpaca API for trading functionality. Alpaca may collect data (e.g., account details, trading activity) as part of their service. We are not responsible for Alpaca’s data practices. Review Alpaca’s Privacy Policy at https://alpaca.markets/disclosures.

8. Data Retention

No data is retained by the Application, as none is collected or stored.

9. Your Rights

Since the Application does not collect or store data, we do not process requests for data access, correction, or deletion. For data handled by Alpaca, contact Alpaca directly to exercise applicable data rights.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not collect or process any data from minors.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted at https://oauth.codeedge.com.br/privacy-policy.html. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at azc@blinq.capital.

Disclaimer: blinq Capital LLC is not responsible for the data practices of Alpaca Securities LLC. Alpaca is a FINRA and SIPC member. For details on how Alpaca handles your data, see https://alpaca.markets/disclosures.
